GRIEVANCE

The college has a Grievance system through which grievance of students as well as faculty if any can be reported to dedicated committee constituted from the senior professors of the institute and remedial measures are taken by them within minimum duration.


A Grievance is any discontent or dissatisfaction, whether expressed or not, whether valid or not, arising out of anything that is directly connected to the institute, and which a student/faculty thinks, believes, or even feels, is unfair, unjust or inequitable.
